My Neighborhood

Welcome to my neighborhood! Considering I live in a super small town, the whole town is my neighborhood. The people who live here are very kind and hardworking. There are lots of farms with horses and lots of cattle. It is a tight knit community that will always look out for you.

    Whipples Country Store

  • The only restaurant/store we have in town!
  • Serves typical American foods, such as burgers and fries.
  • Also has a store section, where you can get snacks and basic groceries.
